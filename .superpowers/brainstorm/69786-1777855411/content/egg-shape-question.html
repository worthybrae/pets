Egg Shape Variants

The base egg silhouette — how organic vs. geometric should the shapes be?

Organic

Classic egg shape — smooth, natural curves. Different sizes (round, elongated, squat). Feels biological, alive.

Geometric / Crystalline

Faceted, gem-like eggs with angular surfaces. Different poly counts by rarity. Feels mystical, artifact-like.

Hybrid

Organic egg silhouette with geometric scale/facet patterns on the surface. Best of both — natural form, detailed texture.

All options have the same beating heart core. Shape variety comes from proportions (tall/squat/round) and surface detail.
